Ramen

Description:

Simply put, ramen is a Japanese noodle soup, with a combination of a rich flavoured broth, one of a variety of types of noodle and a selection of meats or vegetables, often topped with a boiled egg.

Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Broth
  • Vegetables
  • Egg
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chicken

Cooking Instructions:

  1. 1. Add the meat
  2. 2 cups of Soy Sauce
  3. 1/2 cup of Water
  4. Grate the ginger
  5. Mince the garlic with a knife. Mincing is to cut into very fine, even pieces using a sharp knife.
  6. Cut the serrano [AL1] pepper into small chunks
  7. Chopped the onion into small chunks
  8. Add all the ingredients above in a pot
  9. Set the heat to low and let it sit for an hour
